Featured Recipe: Homemade Lasagna

This month's highlight is a classic homemade lasagna that everyone will love!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned; add marinara sauce and simmer.
  4. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and finish with Parmesan on top.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
